FAQ

Questions farmers ask first

Is KrishiGyan free?

Yes. Core scheme discovery, calculators, and guidance content are designed to be free for farmers.

Are schemes official?

KrishiGyan organizes information from government and public sources, but users should verify final eligibility on official portals or local departments.

Can I apply through this site?

The portal can guide you to the right scheme and documents. Direct application support can be added where official online links are available.

Is my data safe?

Lead forms should collect only necessary details and clearly explain how contact information is used.

Which states are supported?

The directory is ready for all Indian states and union territories, with state-wise pages and filters.
